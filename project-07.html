Context & Methodology
Problem
A small retail outlet in Madurai was managing 12 product categories purely on intuition — ordering when shelves looked low. The result: simultaneous overstock of slow-moving items and stockouts of fast-moving ones, tying up ₹4.2 lakh in suboptimal inventory.
Result
Applying EOQ + ROP + Safety Stock reduced optimal inventory value to ₹2.5 lakh (39.8% reduction). Stockout events dropped by 83% in simulation. Projected annual savings from reduced holding cost and eliminated emergency procurement: ₹1.69 lakh.
