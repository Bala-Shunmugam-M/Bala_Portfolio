About me

My Value Story

I'm a dual-background professional — B.E. in Electrical & Electronics Engineering and MBA in Operations Management. This combination lets me go beyond textbook frameworks: I understand the electrical and mechanical side of production systems, and I can map that to operational metrics, cost structures, and improvement opportunities.

During my internship at Agni Steels, I observed how a single bottleneck at the cooler stage rippled across the entire production line — causing downstream idle time, missed targets, and energy waste. That experience showed me that operations is where engineering knowledge creates direct business impact.

I approach problems with process mapping, data analysis, and a bias toward simple, measurable solutions — not overcomplicated frameworks.
