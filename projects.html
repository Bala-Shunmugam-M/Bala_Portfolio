04 — B.E. Final Year · Power Systems · EEE Simulation
B.E. EEE · Final Year Python + MATLAB Power Systems · Protection 08 / 08

Adaptive Protection Relay Coordination for Gas Turbine Generator — VGTPS

Simulation-validated adaptive 87G relay scheme for the GE Frame 6FA generator at Valuthur GTPS — built from actual plant data (51,921 A bus fault level, 172 V bus-bar stability voltage, 4429 A rated current). The adaptive setting detects a high-resistance stator earth fault in 44 ms that the fixed-setting relay completely misses — directly motivated by the real May 2010 stator fault that caused a 3-month outage. Covers 5 protection zones across 10 simulated fault scenarios.
